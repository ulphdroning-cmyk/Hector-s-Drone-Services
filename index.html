About Me

Hi, I’m Hector — I fly cameras.

I help people see familiar places in a brand-new way. From cosy homes and packed festivals to long-term builds, I capture smooth, cinema‑style aerials that tell the story from above.

I started droning because I love that moment when everything clicks into a new perspective. Since then I’ve filmed homes, events, and construction progress around local villages and towns.

  • Experience: Events, Real Estate, Construction
  • Deliverables: 4K video, graded clips, high‑res photos
  • Safety: Pre‑flight checks, insured, flight logs on request
